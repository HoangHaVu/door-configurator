# Design System: Door Configurator
**Project ID:** 18247884583255997473

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The "Door Configurator" design system embodies a **Premium Technical** aesthetic. It blends German engineering precision with high-end architectural elegance. The mood is **Sleek, Authoritative, and Utilitarian**, designed to instill confidence in professional-grade craftsmanship. It uses a high-contrast layout where bold, heavy typography meets airy whitespace and sophisticated material-inspired colors.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Deep Tech Blue (#1271a1):** The primary brand color. Used for dominant call-to-action buttons, interactive icons, and as a bold background for high-impact promotional sections.
* **Dark Iron Slate (#334155):** Used for prominent headings and secondary UI elements to provide a grounded, metallic feel.
* **Muted Steel Gray (#64748b):** The workhorse for descriptive body text and labels, offering excellent readability without competing with the primary brand blue.
* **Pure Arctic White (#ffffff):** The primary canvas color for sections and cards, ensuring a clean and professional presentation of the door configurations.
* **Whisper Gray (#f8fafc):** A very subtle background color used to differentiate UI sections and grouped content areas without adding visual clutter.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** **Inter**, chosen for its modern, clean, and highly legible character, reinforcing the technical nature of the project.
* **Headers:** Display headings use **Black (900) weight** with **tight letter-spacing**, creating an impactful, almost structural presence.
* **Labels:** Functional labels and badges use **Extra-Small (xs) font-size**, **Bold weight**, and **Wide tracking**, all in **Uppercase** to resemble architectural blueprints or technical tags.
* **Body:** General text is set in a balanced medium-to-semibold weight, prioritizing clarity and professional tone.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Primary Buttons:** Softly rounded corners (`rounded-xl`), heavy weight typography (`font-black`), and pronounced premium elevation (`shadow-2xl`) using a subtle black tint. They feature a smooth transition to a slightly lighter surface on hover.
* **Informational Cards:** Generously rounded corners (`rounded-2xl`) with whisper-soft diffused shadows (`shadow-sm`) and a thin, precise border (`border-slate-100`). On hover, these cards elevate further with a stronger, sophisticated depth shadow.
* **Feature Icons:** Housed in rounded-xl containers with a very light tint of the primary blue (10% opacity), emphasizing the specific functionality (e.g., security, thermal).

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace Strategy:** Ample vertical padding (`py-24`) is used between sections to allow the technical details of the door configurations to "breathe" and feel premium.
* **Grid Alignment:** A strict 12-column grid system is used, with content centered in a `max-w-7xl` container to ensure focus and organized visual hierarchy.
* **Responsive Density:** Layouts transition from single-column vertical stacks on mobile to spacious 3-column grids on desktop, maintaining a consistent sense of order and structure.
